About YMU

YMU is a lanchpad written in python. It is pleasing to look at thanks to utilizing the CustomTkinter-library.

The project aims to simplify the updating- and injecting-process of YimMenu.

It all started with an Idea and a post in the YimMenu's GitHub-Community-Section (deleted since DMCA strike against Yimura).

After many updates YMU is now a fully fledged Launchpad with many features for you to try out.

You can manage your Luas in YMU, find new Luas to try and do much more.

What are you waiting for - Try it out now, its better then ever before.

Key Features

Updating/Downloading

Keep your YimMenu always up to date. You get a Notification if an outdated version of YimMenu is detected. Simply click Update/Download in YMU's landing page.

Injecting

Don't worry anymore about finding the Injection-tool that fits your needs, YMU comes with one out of the box. Just open the tab and follow the guide (more info-button)

Endless Guides

If you feel lost then there is a "more info"-button for you in every tab. So you always know how to properly use this tool.
